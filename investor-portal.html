Rio Grande Wilshire Alternative Yield Fund

The Rio Grande Wilshire Alternative Yield Fund is an alternative investment vehicle targeting income-generating strategies with low correlation to public markets, prioritizing capital preservation and consistent returns across market cycles.

Rio Grande Advisors' Role: Rio Grande Advisors serves as Investment Advisor and Special Limited Partner to the fund, bringing institutional-grade portfolio oversight and cross-border expertise to benefit fund investors.

Strategic Partnership — Wilshire Advisors: The fund is structured in partnership with Wilshire Advisors, an investment adviser and manager to some of the world's largest institutions and a full-spectrum provider of alternative investment solutions. Wilshire advises on more than $1.5 trillion in assets and manages $134 billion. *

* Asset figures relate solely to Wilshire Advisors and not to Rio Grande Advisors.
